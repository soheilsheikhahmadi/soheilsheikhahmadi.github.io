About Me

I am Soheil Sheikh Ahmadi, holding a B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering, an M.Sc. in Mechatronics Engineering, and a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering–Control from the University of Tabriz. I am also the CEO of Vira Fanavar Toural Company, where I lead research and development in smart energy control systems and educational robotics.

My research and professional interests lie in the areas of advanced control systems including switching control, model predictive control, and robust control alongside machine learning, reinforcement learning, and robotics. Over the years, I have been engaged in projects that bridge theory and practice, from intelligent control of robotic platforms to applying optimization and AI-driven methods in real-world engineering problems.

I am passionate about developing intelligent, adaptive, and resilient systems that can learn, interact, and perform effectively in uncertain and dynamic environments. My vision is to contribute to innovative solutions that not only advance scientific knowledge but also create meaningful impacts in robotics, healthcare, energy systems, and beyond.

Research & Interests

  • Switching, robust, and model predictive control methods for complex dynamical systems.
  • Machine learning and reinforcement learning algorithms, with applications in robotics and intelligent decision-making.
  • Robotics and mechatronics, including parallel robot control, mobile robots, and autonomous systems.
  • Optimization techniques for improving efficiency and resilience in energy systems and intelligent engineering applications.
