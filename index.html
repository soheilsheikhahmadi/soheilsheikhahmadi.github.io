Control Systems • Robotics • Machine Learning

I am Soheil Sheikh Ahmadi, holding a B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering, an M.Sc. in Mechatronics Engineering, and a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering–Control from the University of Tabriz. I am also the CEO of Vira Fanavar Toural, where I lead R&D in smart energy control systems and educational robotics.

My research spans advanced control (switching/impulsive, MPC, robust control), machine learning and reinforcement learning, and robotics—bridging theory and practice to build intelligent, adaptive, and resilient systems for real‑world environments in robotics, healthcare, and energy.
